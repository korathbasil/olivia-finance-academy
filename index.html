Courses we provide

ACCA

The ACCA course or qualification is offered globally by the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants of UK, a global body for professional accountants. It is a fast growing international accountancy organization with a flexible examination scheme, which makes the course more attractive. ACCA provides a strong foundation to students for careers in Accounting, Tax Consulting, and Auditing.
